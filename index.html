Stellaris Random Empire Generator

This app will generate a random empire for Stellaris, taking into account any exclusive combinations and requirements. There will sometimes be species trait points to spare, which can be used later in the game when genetically engineering your species. This means that some randomly-generated species will have bad starts, but will have many options open to them when the appropriate technologies have been researched.

If you'd like some ideas for naming your species, homeworld, empire etc. then consider visiting Fantasy Name Generators. There are over 1000 name generators on the site, many of which I've found useful for Stellaris.
